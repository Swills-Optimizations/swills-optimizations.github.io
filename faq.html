What is Swills?

Swills is an optimization service aimed at maximizing your system’s performance through improving FPS, lowering latency and reducing input lag.

How much performance gains can I expect?

Realistically, in all cases we can guarantee a 2x FPS boost in Minecraft and similar results in other games. If you have a lot of clutter, junk and unnecessary bloatware on your PC, you can expect a lot more. You can also expect improvements to input lag as well as an uplift in your internet connection.

Is Swills safe?

Our tweaks have been tested accordingly on several devices resulting in a fool-proof service. If however customers feel insecure regarding our optimizations, a RESTORE POINT is always created prior to the optimization service resulting in the customer being able to revert in the case of them wishing to do so.

Why was Swills founded?

We created Swills to aid gamers, content creators and general PC users in unleashing untapped computing potential. Our ideology revolves around getting the best performance from what you have and thus we wish to provide as much help and tweaks as possible without customers needing to upgrade hardware.
